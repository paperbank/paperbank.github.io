Love Frankie is a Bangkok-based social change creative agency. We respond to challenging social and development issues affecting people across Asia. We believe that innovative social networking and entertaining media content has the power to change lives. Love Frankie is a part of the M&C Saatchi World Services global network.

EVIDENCE

Love Frankie takes an evidence-based, impact-driven approach. We employ robust social and behaviour change methodologies and data collection practices to inform our strategy development process.

PARTICIPATION

Participation underpins all aspects of our work. We actively seek the involvement of target populations, opinion leaders, influencers and celebrities in pursuing sustainable social change.

INNOVATION

We harness the power of innovative technologies and media genres to create dynamic social networks that stimulate active engagement with a wide range of contemporary issues.

COLLABORATION

We engage with a wide range of partners including bilateral and multi-lateral donors, government agencies, trusts, foundations and private sector companies to identify opportunities for maximum impact, visibility and reach.

MEET THE TEAM

Matt Love and Rebecca ‘Frankie’ Mok are leading social movement builders in the Asia Pacific region with over 10 years experience in mobilising populations around critical social issues. As the leaders of the highly successful MTV EXIT campaign that educated millions of people worldwide about the issue of modern day slavery through innovative live events, TV programs, digital content and youth outreach programs, their work in Asia has been heralded as providing one of the most comprehensive platforms for engagement on human rights globally.
